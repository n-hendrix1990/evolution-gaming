Events and Times

  • Monday- Cards Against Monday Blues- A Cards Against Humanity Event

  • Wednesday- Magic The Gathering Tournaments

Network

Meet players, vendors, and lots of great people through our events!

  • Vendors are always welcome to attend events as guests. Networking is encouraged, please no selling on premises.
  • Have fun competing and cooperating with other players and guests.
  • Players can form and register as solo or team players.
  • Come have fun meeting and interacting with other attendees!

Records!

Competitions will include the wins and loss ratios, as well as high-score values of participating players on a month-long basis. At the end of each month, players will have the option to:

  • Contribute and save accumulated points towards their membership account
  • Use their points for in-store products
  • Receive a mystery gift through the expenditure of their previous month's point accumukation (additional points on account will be exempt)
